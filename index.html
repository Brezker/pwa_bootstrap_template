Day of the Dead History

The Day of the Dead is a traditional Mexican celebration that honors loved ones who have passed away. During this festival, it is believed that the souls of the deceased return to the world of the living to enjoy of the offerings that are prepared for them. Offerings usually include food, drinks, and objects that the deceased appreciated in life.

Halloween History

Halloween is a holiday of Celtic origin that is celebrated mainly in Anglo-Saxon countries. During this night, spirits and supernatural creatures are believed to roam the Earth. The people He dresses up as scary creatures and goes from house to house asking for candy in an act known as trick or treating.
